Selection from recent literature¶

You will find below a personal selection (hand-picked!) from recent scientific literature organized in three main topics:

  • Spintronics: research in magnetic semiconductors plus magnetoresistive- and organic-based spintronics.
  • Synchrotron: research with synchrotron radiation (mainly focused on core-level spectroscopies) plus techniques for data collection and analysis based on x-rays (synchrotron but also laboratory sources).
  • SciColLab (Scientific Collaborative Laboratory): (open source) computer tools to help in everyday research work (e.g. programming, bibliography management, scientific software) and discussion about open science in the era of Web 2.0.

Do not hesitate to follow it in your RSS reader or via Email (see links below) and, most important, to send me your comments/feedback.
