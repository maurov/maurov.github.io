Personal selection from recent literature

You will find below a personal selection (hand-picked!) from scientific literature organized in three main topics:

  • Spintronics: mainly research in magnetic semiconductors but also magnetoresistive- and organic-based spintronics.
  • Synchrotron: techniques for data collection and analysis based on x-rays (synchrotron but also laboratory sources).
  • SciColLab (Scientific Collaborative Laboratory): (open source) computer tools to help in everyday research work (e.g. programming, bibliography management, scientific software) and discussion about open science in the era of Web 2.0.
